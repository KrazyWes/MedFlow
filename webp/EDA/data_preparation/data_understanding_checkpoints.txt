MedFlow — CRISP-DM — Data Understanding Checkpoints (TXT)
Source: CRISP-DM/data_preparation.py
Updated to align with step labels (01–06) and EDA structure (steps/)

===============================================================================
WHAT EACH SNAPSHOT PRINTS
===============================================================================
- Rows/Cols
- Dtypes (full map if <= 60 cols; otherwise a count)
- Key columns availability (when provided)
- Duplicate rows count
- Missing values count (top N columns)
- Numeric summary (describe) for numeric columns

===============================================================================
EDA OUTPUT (steps/)
===============================================================================
  steps/01_load_raw/DOH, PhilGEPS
  steps/02_fix_data_types/DOH
  steps/03_filter_relevant_data/PhilGEPS
  steps/05_handle_missing_values/DOH, PhilGEPS

===============================================================================
CHECKPOINT MAP (DOH)
===============================================================================
DOH raw_datasets/DOH/ (CSV or Excel)

1) [01 Load Raw] After loading each CSV or Excel sheet
   Label: "DOH raw (CSV) - <filename>" or "DOH raw (Excel) - <filename> / sheet '<sheet>'"
   Purpose: Verify expected columns; inspect missingness/duplicates before cleaning

2) [02 Fix Data Types] After clean_doh_dataframe + adding Year (per file/sheet)
   Label: "DOH after header/currency cleaning - ..."
   Purpose: Confirm column normalization, currency as numeric, Year column

3) [04 Remove Duplicates] Before imputation
   Process: drop_duplicates() on full row (no separate snapshot; applied before step 5)

4) [05 Handle Missing Values] After imputation
   Label: "DOH merged (after missing-value handling)"
   Purpose: Confirm computed fields (TOTAL=Q*UNIT_COST); confirm no remaining missing

5) [06 Final Clean] Export to this_datasets/doh_medicine_distribution_2022_2025.csv

===============================================================================
CHECKPOINT MAP (PhilGEPS)
===============================================================================
PhilGEPS raw_datasets/PhilGEPS/ (CSV or Excel)

1) [01 Load Raw] After loading each CSV or Excel sheet
   Label: "PhilGEPS raw (CSV) - <filename>" or "PhilGEPS raw (Excel) - ..."
   Purpose: Verify key columns; inspect missingness/duplicates before filtering

2) [03 Filter Relevant Data] After medical keyword filter (UNSPSC Description)
   Label: "PhilGEPS filtered to medical records (before missing-value handling)"
   Purpose: Validate filter result size and missingness

3) [04 Remove Duplicates] Before imputation
   Process: drop_duplicates() on full row

4) [05 Handle Missing Values] After imputation
   Label: "PhilGEPS filtered to medical records (after missing-value handling)"
   Purpose: Confirm numeric/categorical imputations

5) [06 Final Clean] Export to this_datasets/philgeps_2025_medical_procurement.csv

6) Fallback (if 'UNSPSC Description' absent):
   Label: "PhilGEPS unfiltered (after missing-value handling)"
   Export: philgeps_2025_medical_procurement_unfiltered.csv
